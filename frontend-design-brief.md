# Lightpath Coaching Frontend Design Brief

## Core Positioning

Lightpath Coaching is a New Zealand-style education and tutoring website focused on confidence, personalised learning, and steady progress.

The website should feel warm, trustworthy, calm, and professional. The main message is not only academic improvement, but helping children feel confident, capable, and supported.

Primary message:

> Confidence Starts Here.

Supporting message:

> Where personalised learning helps children feel confident, capable, and proud of their progress.

## Recommended Site Structure

### 1. Home

Purpose: Make parents immediately understand the emotional value of the service and guide them to book a conversation.

Recommended sections:

1. Hero
   - Full-width natural photo of confident, happy children in a New Zealand learning context.
   - Strong heading: "Confidence Starts Here."
   - Supporting copy.
   - Primary CTA: "Book Now" or "Book a Free Chat".

2. Three Value Pillars
   - More Than Grades.
   - A Place to Build Confidence.
   - Discover What You're Capable Of.
   - Keep each explanation short and parent-focused.

3. Transformation Section
   - Heading: From "I can't" to "I can."
   - Show the emotional journey: uncertainty, support, confidence, progress.
   - This could become a horizontal step layout or a two-column story block.

4. Parent Testimonials
   - Heading: What Parents Say
   - 3-4 testimonial cards.
   - If screenshots are real and readable, use them carefully to build trust.

5. Final CTA
   - Heading: Let's Help Your Child Feel Confident Again.
   - Button: Book a Free Chat.

6. Footer
   - Logo / name.
   - Phone.
   - Email.
   - Instagram.
   - Facebook.

### 2. Tutoring

Purpose: Explain the tutoring process clearly so parents feel the organisation is structured and professional.

Recommended sections:

1. Page Header
   - Heading: Personalised Tutoring That Builds Confidence.
   - Short intro: explain that lessons are tailored to the child's needs, level, and learning style.

2. How It Works
   - Step 1: Get to Know Your Child
   - Step 2: Understand Their Learning Needs
   - Step 3: Create a Personalised Plan
   - Step 4: Learn and Grow Every Week
   - Use numbered cards or a vertical timeline.

3. Subjects
   - Display all subjects offered.
   - Recommended card fields: subject name, year levels, format, short benefit.

4. CTA
   - Book a Free Chat.

### 3. Meet Our Tutors

Purpose: Build trust by showing real people, experience, and teaching personality.

Recommended sections:

1. Page Header
   - Heading: Meet Our Tutors
   - Short intro focused on supportive, experienced, student-centred teaching.

2. Tutor Cards
   - Photo.
   - Name.
   - Subjects.
   - Teaching experience.
   - Teaching strengths.
   - Short personal teaching style.

Recommended fields to highlight:

- Subjects taught.
- Year levels.
- Years of experience.
- Teaching style.
- Strengths, such as patience, exam prep, confidence building, explaining difficult concepts.
- Optional: language support, curriculum familiarity, student outcomes.

### 4. About

Purpose: Explain why Lightpath exists and why parents should trust it.

Recommended sections:

1. Our Story
   - Brief human story about why the organisation was created.

2. Our Mission
   - Helping students feel confident, supported, and capable through personalised learning.

3. What Makes Us Different
   - Confidence, not just scores.
   - Tailored lessons based on individual needs.
   - Small group support and personalised tracking.
   - Free initial learning assessment.

4. Our Approach
   - Supportive learning environment.
   - NZ curriculum alignment.
   - Regular feedback to parents.
   - Progress tracking.

### 5. Contact / Booking

Purpose: Make conversion simple.

This should be a separate page, even if the main CTA appears on every page.

Recommended fields:

- Parent name.
- Email.
- Phone.
- Child year level.
- Subject interest.
- Main concern or goal.
- Preferred contact method.

## Navigation Recommendation

Desktop navigation:

- Home
- Tutoring
- Meet Our Tutors
- About
- Book a Free Chat

Mobile navigation:

- Use a simple menu button.
- Keep "Book a Free Chat" visible or prominent.

## Visual Direction

The website should feel like a New Zealand education service:

- Clean, bright, natural.
- Calm white background with soft green, blue, or warm yellow accents.
- Real photography preferred over generic illustrations.
- Avoid overly corporate, dark, or tech-heavy styling.
- Use generous spacing and clear headings.
- Make mobile layout a first-class experience.

Recommended visual mood:

- Trustworthy.
- Warm.
- Parent-friendly.
- Professional but not cold.
- Optimistic but not childish.

## Content Notes

The current idea is strong and coherent. The clearest brand angle is:

> Personalised tutoring that helps children build confidence.

The site should consistently repeat three themes:

1. Confidence.
2. Personalised support.
3. Visible progress.

Avoid making the website sound like every other tutoring centre by only talking about grades, exams, and subjects. Those are important, but the emotional parent concern is whether their child feels lost, stressed, behind, or discouraged.

## Frontend MVP Scope

For the first version, build:

- Home page.
- Tutoring page.
- Meet Our Tutors page.
- About page.
- Contact / booking page.
- Shared header.
- Shared footer.
- Reusable cards for values, process steps, subjects, tutors, and testimonials.

Do not build login, admin, database, or payments in the first frontend pass.
